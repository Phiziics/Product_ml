# Product ML Portfolio

This repository contains product-focused machine learning and data science projects organized by product category.

The goal is to show how Python, SQL, experimentation, causal inference, forecasting, recommender systems, retention modeling, and dashboards can support product growth and business decisions.

## Categories

### A/B Testing and Experimentation
Projects focused on product experiments, metric design, causal inference, statistical testing, and launch decision-making.

### Recommender Systems
Projects focused on ranking, personalization, collaborative filtering, and product discovery.

### Retention Modeling
Projects focused on churn prediction, cohort analysis, activation, engagement, and lifecycle analytics.

### Forecasting
Projects focused on demand forecasting, usage forecasting, time series modeling, and business planning.

### Anomaly Detection
Projects focused on detecting metric drops, performance regressions, fraud, or unusual product behavior.

### Pricing Optimization
Projects focused on revenue strategy, elasticity, conversion, and monetization decisions.
